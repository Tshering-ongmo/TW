Article by Kezang Dorji

Sherubtse to me is what Hogwarts is to Harry Potter, Keazang Dorji

Sherubtse to me is what Hogwarts is to Harry Potter. It is what shaped me into the person I am today. Many of the best things in my life happened or started there. Its enabling environment makes people, as in my case, realize their potential to do what they dream of doing and the difference they can make in the society.

When I joined Sherubtse in 2009 one rule that all freshers (boys in particular) had to follow was to wear Gho all the time for the first month of the first semester. Seniors would come for inspection to individual rooms. So, all freshers had to wear Gho from morning till we went to bed. But whatâ€™s funny is that wearing Gho was the first thing we did after we woke up. We had to have it on even when we went to wash our face or when we visited the room. I used to diligently follow that and to be safe I used to wear my Gho even when I slept. It was a scary experience at that time but today it is something I have fun recollecting.

As the FINA President I revived some old and introduced some new initiatives to make life in college more enriching and interesting. I remember fellow students coming to me to thank me and express their appreciation for the steps I had taken as it benefitted them. This was a great motivation to dedicate myself for a greater cause which I continued doing even after graduation.

Sherubtse has been and will continue to be the premier learning institute in Bhutan which does not confine its students to academic learning but also nurtures their self & social leadership. Sherubtse will sustain as the number one supplier of motivated young people in the country.
