Mailing official transcripts

  • An electronic copy of the transcripts will be sent by the college at no cost.
  • For hard copies of the transcripts, duplicate transcript fees and postage charges apply for mailing
  • Please send your request to records.sherubtse@rub.edu.bt along with a scanned copy of your transcript.

Please be aware that the processing time is 3-5 working days, and it may extend further during vacation periods. Please consider this timeframe when making plans.
