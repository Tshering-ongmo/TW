Sherubtse Timeline

1968

Sherubtse is Born

Sherubtse High School established, inaugurated by the Third King Jigme Dorji Wangchuck.

1976

Junior College Started

Junior College started with 23 students.

1983

Undergraduate Courses Started

Bachelor's Degree courses started with 8 students enrolled in B.A, B.Com., and BSc programs.

2004

Joined RUB

Sherubtse joins other colleges to establish the Royal University of Bhutan (RUB).

2006

Started Degree Programmes

Sherubtse College started offering degree programmes under the Royal University of Bhutan in Arts, Science, and Commerce.

2008

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Math and Physics, BSc in Math and Chemistry, and BSc in Physics and Chemistry were introduced. The College also established the Center for Population and Development Studies (CPDS).

2009

Introduced Double Subject Degree Programmes

BA in Political Science and Sociology, BA in History and Dzongkha, and BA in Dzongkha and English as double subject degree programmes were introduced.

2011

Established a New Research Centre

The Centre for Climate Change and Spatial Information (CCCSI) was established.

2014

Introduced Two New Research Centres

Established two more new Centres of Excellence: Centre for Archeology and Historical Research (CAHR) and Centre for Science and Environmental Research (CSER).

2017

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Mathematics programme was introduced under the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science. BSc in Physics and BSc in Chemistry programmes were introduced under the Department of Physical Science. BA in Population and Development Studies programme was introduced in the Department of Social Science as a single subject degree programme.

2019

Introduced Single Degree Programmes

BA in Economics, BA in English, BA in Media Studies, BA in History, and BSc in Geography programme were introduced as single subject degree programmes.

2021

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Data Science and BSc in Statistics programmes were introduced in the Department of Mathematical Sciences.
