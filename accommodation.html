On-campus Accommodation

Sherubtse has 20 on-campus residential accommodations (10 female residences and 10 male residences) with free unlimited Wi-Fi facilities. Students admitted on Government scholarship receive a monthly stipend of Nu. 2, 500 of which Nu.250 is deducted every month towards rent for accommodation.

Accommodation on campus is of two types:

Centrally Catered Residence Halls:

There are three centrally catered residence halls for females and two for males. Meals are provided from the common mess. All Year 1 students are allocated centrally catered residence halls during their first year. Students on Government scholarships residing in centrally catered residence halls are not paid stipend since Nu.2250 is used for providing meals and other miscellaneous expenditures incurred on running the mess daily and Nu. 250 is deducted as rent. Self-financed students scholarship residing in centrally catered residence halls will have to pay Nu. 2250 per month as mess expenses.

Self-catering Residence Halls

All students after completing Year 1 are allocated self-catering residence halls. There are sixteen self-catering residence halls: 8 for male students and 8 for female students. Students have to manage meals on their own and are paid Nu. 2250 as stipend after deducting Nu.250 as rent.
